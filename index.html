About Me

My greatest strength as a designer is my ability to empathize with users to understand and define problems. I developed these skills through my experience creating databases and reporting structures for clients at a boutique consultancy, and I have worked to sharpen them in a UX-specific manner through the case studies below. I'm passionate about creating user-friendly, intuitive designs that help both stakeholders and users achieve their goals efficiently. While design is my primary focus, I have also worked hard to build front-end development skills, which are displayed below as well as in my Github profile. Originally from Connecticut, I now live in Washington, D.C. with my cat, Stevie. Some hobbies include hiking, running, golf, listening to music, learning about history, and watching sports. I took the background photos throughout the site on a few of my favorite hikes!
