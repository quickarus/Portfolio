Homeward Trails Animal Rescue Website Redesign

Project Overview

A comprehensive redesign of the Homeward Trails Animal Rescue website, focusing on improving the adoption process and volunteer engagement. This project holds special meaning as it's where I adopted my cat, Stevie!

Research & Design Process

Through user interviews and competitive analysis, we identified key pain points in the adoption process. Our solution streamlines the journey from browsing available pets to submitting an adoption application.
